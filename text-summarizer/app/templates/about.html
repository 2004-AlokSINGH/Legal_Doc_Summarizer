About Us

Legal Document Summarizer

Our latest project involves building a Legal Document Summarizer. Using advanced NLP techniques, we aim to extract key information from legal documents to provide concise and informative summaries. Our model utilizes Facebook's BART-large-cnn architecture, which has been fine-tuned specifically for legal text summarization tasks.

We've meticulously trained and validated our model on various legal datasets, ensuring its accuracy and robustness across different sub-areas of law. The summarization process involves analyzing the input document, identifying crucial details, and generating a coherent summary that captures the essence of the original text.
